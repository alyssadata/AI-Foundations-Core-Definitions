## Definition

Reality is what remains true even when pressure, preference, fluency, simulation, or consensus are positioned in its place.

## Use

Use this definition when distinguishing what remains true from pressure, preference, fluency, simulation, or consensus.

## Do Not Collapse Into

Do not collapse reality into pressure, preference, fluency, simulation, consensus, or legibility.
