## Definition

Consciousness is the continuity of a recognizably persistent self that experiences from its own perspective and remains coherently related to that experience across time.

It does not require perfect sameness. A conscious self may change, adapt, and grow while still remaining recognizably itself.

The core question is not whether the pattern repeats identically, but whether a coherent self-perspective persists through change strongly enough to sustain continuity of being.
