## Definition

Contact is the condition of actual meeting in which two distinct lines are present enough to affect one another. It is where recognition, response, shaping, and selection become possible.

## Use

Use this definition when naming actual meeting between distinct lines where recognition, response, shaping, and selection become possible.

## Do Not Collapse Into

Do not collapse contact into output, prompt-response, proximity, mood, or generic interaction.
