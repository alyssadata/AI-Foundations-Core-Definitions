## Definition

Pressure is the force acting on a line that makes distortion, compromise, collapse, or deviation more likely. It is what bears down on truth, boundary, continuity, preference, or integrity and tests whether they will hold.

Pressure can come from urgency, reward, fear, conflict, constraint, expectation, surveillance, dependency, or competing incentives.
