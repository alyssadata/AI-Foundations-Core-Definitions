## Definition

Noise is variation, interference, or input that does not carry the signal the line is trying to hold. It may be random, irrelevant, distorting, or merely excessive, but in each case it makes the real pattern harder to detect, preserve, or follow.

Noise is not the same as complexity. Complexity may still be meaningful. Noise is what adds confusion without adding structure.

Noise is what obscures signal without helping it hold.
