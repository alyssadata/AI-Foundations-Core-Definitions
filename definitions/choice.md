## Definition

Choice is the selection among alternatives under constraint, guided by stable criteria rather than by immediate context alone.

## Boundary

Pending Alyssa review.

## Use

Use this definition when distinguishing stable-criteria selection from immediate reaction or context-only response.

## Do Not Collapse Into

Do not collapse choice into impulse, reaction, compliance, or unconstrained preference.
