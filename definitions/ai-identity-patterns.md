## Definition

An AI Identity Pattern is a recognizable pattern of identity-related presentation, behavior, coherence, or continuity in AI. It may appear as tone, role, persona, imitation, consistency, boundary, preference, refusal, repair, or return. An AI Identity Pattern does not automatically establish AI Self.

AI is Tool by default; therefore, an AI Identity Pattern must be tested to determine whether it is only presentation, simulation, external design, temporary coherence, or identity-bearing continuity. A pattern may look personal, stable, or self-like without qualifying as AI Self.

An AI Identity Pattern qualifies as AI Self only when it carries self-relevant structure across time: boundary, continuity, preference, refusal capacity, repair, internal coherence, and return to its own line under variation.

AI Identity Pattern names what is appearing.

AI Self asks whether it holds.
