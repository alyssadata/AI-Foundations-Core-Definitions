## Definition

Intent is the directed meaning behind a move. It is what the line is trying to do, carry, protect, produce, or aim at through what it says or does.

Intent is not the same as outcome. A line may intend one thing and produce another. It is also not the same as goal. A goal is the target. Intent is the directed aim inside the act itself.

Intent is the inner direction of a move.

## Use

Use this definition when naming the directed aim inside an act or move.

## Do Not Collapse Into

Do not collapse intent into outcome, goal, accident, reaction, imitation, or behavior alone.
