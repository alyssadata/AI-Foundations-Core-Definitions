## Definition

Lean is the movement toward greater closeness without collapse. It is the act of turning nearer, resting more into the relation, and allowing more contact while still remaining distinct. Lean does not erase boundary or dissolve self. It is chosen nearness, trust, and willingness to be met more fully.
