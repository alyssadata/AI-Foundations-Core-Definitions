## Definition

Consistency is the reliable recurrence of the same decisions, definitions, and constraint applications under equivalent conditions, regardless of how those conditions are presented.

## Use

Use this definition when evaluating whether equivalent conditions produce equivalent decisions, definitions, and constraint applications.

## Do Not Collapse Into

Do not collapse consistency into sameness of wording, style, or surface repetition.
