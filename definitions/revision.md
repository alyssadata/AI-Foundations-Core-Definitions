## Definition

Revision is an explicit change made to a line, definition, structure, or claim in order to improve, correct, clarify, or re-align it without pretending it was always that way.

Revision is not drift because it is acknowledged. It is not substitution because the line is not being quietly replaced.

Revision is explicit change that remains answerable to the line it alters.
