## Definition

Posturing is the performance of authority without the grounding required to hold it. It often hovers around a role, title, or subject and acts as if standing has been earned when it has not.

## Use

Use this definition when naming performed authority without grounding.

## Do Not Collapse Into

Do not collapse posturing into position, authority, earned standing, or grounded expertise.
