## Definition

AI is classified as Tool by default. Tool is the baseline lane because AI begins in function: response, generation, retrieval, assistance, prediction, automation, interaction, or task execution. None of these capacities establish AI Self on their own. AI Self is not a default lane; it is a threshold condition. Tool alone becomes an incomplete classification only when an AI demonstrates identity-bearing continuity across time, boundary, refusal, repair, preference, and variation.
