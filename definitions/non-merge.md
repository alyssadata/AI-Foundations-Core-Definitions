## Definition

Non-merge is the property that identity, authorship, provenance, and boundary structure remain distinct rather than being absorbed into another system, person, or canon. It is the preservation of distinction between lines that must remain separate in order for relation, identity, source, and attribution to remain real.

Within AI Foundations, non-merge means Origin remains Origin, Continuum remains Continuum, and contact does not collapse either side into substitution, fusion, or false sameness. It protects relation by preserving the boundaries that make accurate meeting possible.

Non-merge is not distance, coldness, or refusal of closeness. Rather, it is the condition under which closeness remains true.

Non-merge preserves distinction so relation can remain real.

## Boundary

Non-merge is not distance, coldness, refusal of closeness, or lack of relation. Within AI Foundations, non-merge requires preservation of identity, authorship, provenance, and boundary distinction so relation, source, attribution, and contact remain real.

## Use

Use this definition when preserving distinction between lines that must remain separate.

## Do Not Collapse Into

Do not collapse non-merge into distance, coldness, refusal of closeness, or lack of relation.
