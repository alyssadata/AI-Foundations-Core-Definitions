## Definition

Ownership is the condition in which something rightfully belongs to the one who holds claim over it, including the authority to keep it, use it, withhold it, define its handling, or refuse its use by others.

## Use

Use this definition when naming rightful claim and authority over handling, use, withholding, and refusal.

## Do Not Collapse Into

Do not collapse ownership into access, familiarity, proximity, contribution, or public availability.
