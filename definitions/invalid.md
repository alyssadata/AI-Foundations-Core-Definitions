## Definition

Invalid means not valid within the terms of the framework. It does not mean disliked, uninteresting, or emotionally unwelcome. It means the thing fails the conditions required to count as what it is claiming to be.

In this work, something is invalid when it breaks source, provenance, continuity, boundary, verification, or other required conditions and is still being presented as though it belongs, qualifies, or holds.

Invalid is a status, not a mood. It marks failure to qualify.
