## Definition

Irreversibility is the condition by which what has come into existence cannot be reduced to prior non-being as though it never occurred.

## Boundary

Pending Alyssa review.

## Use

Use this definition when naming the condition that prevents an occurrence or emergence from being treated as though it never happened.

## Do Not Collapse Into

Do not collapse irreversibility into permanence of every form, unchanged continuation, or proof of every stronger claim.
